Field notes

Common questions.

What's new in v2.1?+

Everything that made v1 scripts feel generic is gone. v2.1 learns your voice from your real reels before writing, writes both demo scripts (30–55s) and story scripts (45–90s), uses hooks with real June 2026 view counts, refuses to write thin scripts without real substance, and tells you when to repost a winner.

Do I need a paid Claude plan?+

The skill works on any Claude plan. Cowork requires Pro or above. Claude Code requires Pro or Team. On the free tier the skill still works — you'll just hit usage limits faster.

Does it only work for AI/tech content?+

Not at all. Fitness, cooking, finance, beauty, business, travel, parenting — the voice profile is built from YOUR reels, so it adapts to whatever you make.

Can I use it for sponsored content?+

Yes. Mention it's sponsored in your prompt. v2 opens with the result the product delivers, names the brand by second 5–8, and keeps disclosures in the caption — so it never sounds like an ad.

Does it write captions too?+

Every script comes with a staccato-style caption, one keyword CTA, and 5–8 hashtags — ready to paste into Instagram, TikTok, or your scheduling tool.
